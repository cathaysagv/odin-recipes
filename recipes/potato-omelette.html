Spanish Omelette

Description

I’ll show you how to make an authentic Spanish potato omelette step by step, with the best tips and tricks to make it absolutely delicious!

Ingredients

  • 4 potatoes
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 sweet onion
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt

How to make Spanish Omelette

  1. Start by peeling and slicing the potatoes into evenly sized slices.
  2. Place them in a frying pan with enough olive oil to cover them and let them cook gently over medium-low heat for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally so they don’t stick.
  3. Once cooked, remove and set aside.
  4. Meanwhile, thinly slice the onion (fine julienne) and cook it in a pan with a little oil and a pinch of salt until soft.
  5. When the potatoes and onion are ready, remove and set aside, draining off any excess oil.
  6. Beat the eggs in a bowl, add the potatoes and onion, season with salt to taste, and let the mixture rest for about 5 minutes.
  7. Cook (set) the tortilla in a non-stick pan on both sides for about 45 seconds per side. The exact time depends on how runny or set you like it.
